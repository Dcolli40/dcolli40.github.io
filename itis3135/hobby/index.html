What is Disc Golf?

Disc Golf has some rules that a new player should definitely know!

  • Disc Golf is a lot like regular golf where your score is calculated in relation to the par of the course.
  • Par is something that changes per hole, but for most disc golf courses par is either 3, 4, or 5!
  • That means that the player would have 3, 4, or 5 throws to get their disc into the basket!
  • When players throw their disc they must stand even with or behind where their disc landed.
  • This is where "play it where it lies" comes into effect!
  • There are many ways to throw a disc, forehand, backhand, tomohawk, and scuber.
  • Each of these throws have a different effect on the direction of the disc.
  • It is up to the player to test and see what throw they like best
  • In disc golf, the more spin the better!
  • Always remember to check the sign and see where the basket is, and decide what path you want to take.
  • The hole is finished when the players disc lands in the basket.
  • Players score is calculated by how many shots, over or under par they are!
  • Even, or under par is a very Strong score for a player to finish with!
  • But when you first start, dont get too caught up in how high or low your score is, just enjoy the game!!
