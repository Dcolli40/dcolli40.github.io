Website Evaluation

Here I will compare and contrast a website that has good C.R.A.P principles with a website that has poor C.R.A.P principles.

What is C.R.A.P?

  • C: Contrast
    • Contrast deals with the elements on the page and how it effects readability for users.
    • Contrast deals with the colors of a page compared to the background of the page.
  • R: Repeptition
    • Repeptition visual elements of a design is important to make the website look like a finsihed and polished product.
  • A: Alignment
    • Aligning your webpage is important to make it look organized.
    • Proper alignment has unified margins and spacing thoughout.
  • P: Proximity
    • Proximity deals with how close the elements of a page are with the other elements of the page. This is important so that we can have a modern and organized looking website.

Sushi Iwa's Website

  • Repeptition:This site has some inconsistencys but there is not any clear repetition there.
  • Alignment:The alignment seems off and looks like it was thrown together very quickly without much thought.

Nike's Website

  • Contrast:The contrast of Nike's site is very simple with just using white for the background and black for the text.
    • The simplicity of this makes the shoes that Nike is selling stand out more because they are the only colorful thing on the page.
  • Repeptition:Everything is repeated nicely and there is a clear uniform pattern that the website has. which helps make it aesthetically pleasing to view.
  • Alignment:The alignment and margins are done vey well. Everything has its own place on the site and nothing interrupts the flow of it.
  • Proximity:Everything is spaced out enough and the text does not take away from the point of the site which is selling shoes.
