What Is Body Defense?

Body Defense is when one member of your team positions themselves at the net to try and block an opposing teams spike up into the air. Body Defense utilizes the new Soft Touch Rule which means that if a defender hits a ball that is going in the upwards trajectory, they may hit the ball again. Hitting the ball again is the second hit for the team so the other team member must put the ball on net after a soft touch is used.

Pros

  • Quick returns with good defensive touches
  • Teamwork focused
  • Can be frustrating to hit against if done properly
  • Excellent against drops

Cons

  • Could lose points off hard hits
  • Sometimes teammate might not be ready for the rebound
  • Opposing teams setting can make it difficult
