Project Overview: About Spike Central Station:

  1. Spike Central Station is to be used to teach players how to play Spikeball, show the stats of professional players, and bring greater involvement with the community of Spikeball players.
  2. Intended Users: Spikeball players, either competitive or casual.
  3. This site will contain a lerning to play module, a professional spikeball overview, cool tips and tricks, and tournament information.
